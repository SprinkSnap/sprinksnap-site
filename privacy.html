Privacy Policy

Privacy built for professional beta signups.

SprinkSnap collects only the information needed to manage beta interest, communicate product updates, and learn which Revit fire sprinkler design workflows matter most to fire protection professionals.

Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how SprinkSnap Inc. collects, uses, stores, and protects information submitted through sprinksnap.com. SprinkSnap Inc. was formerly FireAcad Inc. and is building a Revit fire sprinkler design automation platform for NFPA 13 workflows, hydraulic calculations, pipe sizing, material takeoffs, and stock listings.

Information we collect

When you submit a beta waitlist form, we may collect:

  • Your work email address.
  • Your role, such as fire protection engineer, sprinkler designer, contractor, consultant, or other.
  • Your company name if you provide it.
  • Basic technical information needed to process the form, such as submission date, source form, browser, and anti-spam signals.

How we use your information

We use submitted information to:

  • Manage the SprinkSnap beta waitlist.
  • Send launch updates, beta access information, and product announcements.
  • Understand demand from fire protection engineers, designers, contractors, and consultants.
  • Improve the product roadmap for Revit, NFPA 13, hydraulic calculation, and material takeoff workflows.
  • Respond to direct questions or requests.

Formspree form processing

SprinkSnap uses Formspree to process beta waitlist submissions. When you submit a form, your information is sent to Formspree so it can securely capture the request and forward it to SprinkSnap. Formspree may process limited technical information to provide spam protection, form delivery, and submission management.

Analytics

SprinkSnap may use privacy-conscious website analytics, such as Cloudflare Web Analytics, to understand aggregate site usage. This helps us understand page visits, referral sources, and conversion activity such as visits to the success page after a beta form submission.

How we share information

We do not sell personal information. We may share information only with service providers that help operate the website, process forms, manage email communications, protect against spam, or maintain business records.

Data retention

We keep beta waitlist information for as long as needed to manage beta interest, communicate product updates, evaluate product demand, and meet reasonable business or legal requirements. You may request removal at any time by emailing us.

Your choices

You may contact SprinkSnap to:

  • Ask what information we have about you.
  • Correct your email, company, or role information.
  • Request removal from the beta waitlist.
  • Stop receiving launch or product update emails.

Security

We use reasonable safeguards and reputable service providers to protect submitted information. No internet transmission or storage system is guaranteed to be perfectly secure, but we aim to keep collected data limited, relevant, and protected.

Children's privacy

SprinkSnap is intended for professional and business use. It is not directed to children, and we do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as SprinkSnap develops, as the website changes, or as our form and analytics providers change. The latest version will be posted on this page.

Contact

For privacy questions or requests, contact SprinkSnap at info@sprinksnap.com.
